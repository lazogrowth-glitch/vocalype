FAQ

Questions before you try it?

Does Vocalype work without internet?

Yes. All transcription models run locally on your machine. Only optional LLM post-processing requires a connection.

Are my voice recordings sent to the cloud?

No. Audio never leaves your device. Transcription happens entirely locally. Your data stays yours.

Does it work on Mac, Windows and Linux?

Yes. All three platforms are supported natively. Optimized for Apple Silicon, Copilot+ PCs and NPU-capable hardware.

What's the difference between Basic and Premium?

Basic gives you 30 free transcriptions per week with base models. Premium unlocks everything: advanced models, unlimited transcriptions, Command Mode, app context, snippets and more.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, anytime from your billing portal. You keep Premium access until the end of the paid period.

Do I need a credit card for the trial?

No. You get 14 days of full Premium access with no credit card required.
