# Growth Playbook

Vocalype growth should be demo-led.

Strong content shows:

- Speech turning into text
- Text appearing in a real app
- A workflow that is faster than typing
- A clear privacy or offline advantage
- A specific audience pain

Prioritize:

- Developers dictating into VS Code and ChatGPT
- Writers drafting paragraphs without typing
- Students capturing notes quickly
- Professionals writing emails and documents
- People with wrist pain reducing typing strain

Avoid:

- Generic startup updates
- Abstract AI productivity claims
- Hooks that hide the product
- Content without a demo scene

Core metric:

View-to-download click rate.
