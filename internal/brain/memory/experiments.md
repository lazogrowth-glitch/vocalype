# Experiments

Every experiment must include:

- Hypothesis
- Change
- Metric
- Start date
- End date
- Result
- Decision

Decision options:

- keep
- kill
- iterate

Example:

Hypothesis:
A demo video above the fold will increase download clicks.

Change:
Add 20-second demo video to homepage hero.

Metric:
Download button CTR.

Decision:
Keep if CTR increases by at least 20%.
