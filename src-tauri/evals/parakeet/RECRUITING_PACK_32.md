Recruiting-oriented validation pack built from the local speech set.

Focus:

- natural answers
- pauses and reformulations
- interview-style long responses
- noise and weak microphone conditions
- dates and scheduling language
- a small French slice for bilingual handling

Composition:

- 24 English samples
- 8 French samples

Manifest:

- `dataset_manifest_recruiting_32.json`

This pack intentionally excludes the more dev/product-specific samples that were kept only in the legacy manifests.
