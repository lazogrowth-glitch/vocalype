# Friends Voice Pack

This pack is designed to collect real-world speech samples from friends for
Spanish and Portuguese.

The goal is simple:

- record the exact sentence
- keep the exact file name
- send back the audio file
- optionally add a short note about accent / mic / noise

## Instructions To Send

You can copy-paste this to your friends:

```text
Please record these short samples for a speech-to-text app test.

Rules:
- Use your normal voice unless the instruction says otherwise
- Keep the exact file name
- Read the exact sentence once
- Send the audio back as .wav or .m4a
- If possible, also tell me:
  - your accent/region
  - whether the room was quiet or noisy
  - whether you used phone, headset, or laptop mic
```
